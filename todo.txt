Given an arbitrary document:

1) txt2sentencecorpus.pl to get a single-spaced corpus.single
2) C99 corpus.single > corpus.c99
3) doublespace corpus.single > corpus
4) buildmatrix.sh corpus
5) gettopicindices.pl corpus.c99
6) for each consecutive pair x, y of topic indices:
	representative corpus.double x y >> summary
7) for each consecutive pair x,y in summary.i:
	glue x y >> summary
	sort summary
   stop when summary length does not increase, or when length > l
8) getdoc `cat summary`
